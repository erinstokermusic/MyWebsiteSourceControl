  • Drum Set:

    Drum set is one of the many percussion instruments I play. It's also one of the most well known percussion instruments. An average set contains one snare drum, one kick drum, two or three tom drums, a hi-hat, one or two crash cymbals, and one ride cymbal. Playing drum set requires use of all four limbs, which is what makes it one of the hardest percussion instruments.
